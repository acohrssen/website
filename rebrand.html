Tae Kwon Do

Tae Kwon Do is a Korean martial art that is based on kicks and punches. The translation of Tae Kwon Do means kick and punch.

Tae Kwon Do Fun Facts

  • Tae Kwon Do is an Olympic sport.
  • Tae Kwon Do has a belt system which includes white belts, colored belts and finally, black belts.
  • Tae Kwon Do is used in Korean military.
  • Tae Kwon Do uses Korean commands.

Famous People

Famous people who have taken Tae Kwon Do include:
  • Chuck Norris
  • Bruce Lee
  • Muhammad Ali
  • Joe Biden
  • Willie Nelson
